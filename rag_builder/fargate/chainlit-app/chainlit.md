This is the `RAG Builder` app. You can interact with it by asking questions
related to your documents.

## How to use

1. Upload your documents (PDFs, text files, etc.) through the `chainlit`
   interface.
1. Ask questions about the content of your uploaded documents.
1. The `RAG Agent` will retrieve relevant information and generate answers.

## Capabilities

- **Document Ingestion:** Upload various document types.
- **Intelligent Retrieval:** Find answers within your documents.
- **Conversational AI:** Interact naturally with your data.
